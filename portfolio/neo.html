Description

NEO is a non-profit community-driven blockchain project. It utilizes blockchain technology and digital identity to digitize assets and automate the management of digital assets using smart contracts. Using a distributed network, it aims to create a “Smart Economy”. This vision provides decentralized applications, payment networks, and scalability that will pave the way for the future of blockchain.
